ABOUT THE FINAL ACT...



Night of the Murder:

Maria De Luca was found dead in her luxurious Hollywood Hills mansion. The discovery was made by her personal assistant, who arrived at the residence after Maria failed to show up for a scheduled event. Maria's lifeless body was discovered in the grand foyer of her mansion, sprawled on the marble floor beneath the majestic chandelier. The cause of Maria's death was determined to be a single gunshot wound to the chest. The murder weapon, a small caliber handgun, was found near her body, indicating a close-range confrontation. The mansion showed signs of struggle, with overturned furniture and shattered glass adding to the scene's chaos. The discovery of Maria's body sent shockwaves through Hollywood, sparking a high-profile investigation that would uncover the dark secrets lurking beneath the glittering facade of the entertainment industry.
