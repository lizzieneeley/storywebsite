THE CRIME SCENE

Below is the crime scene where Maria De Luca was murdered. Move your cursor along the image, and you might find a clue!

Isabella's fingerprints are found on the murder weapon,
indicating that she handled the gun before or after the crime. Furthermore,
surveillance footage shows Isabella entering Maria's mansion shortly before
the time of the murder, providing evidence of her presence at the scene.

Forensic analysis reveals traces of gunpowder residue
on Vincent's clothing, suggesting that he recently fired a weapon.
Additionally, witnesses report hearing heated arguments between
Vincent and Maria in the days leading up to her murder,
raising suspicions about their volatile relationship.

Marco's DNA is discovered under Maria's fingernails,
suggesting that she fought back against her attacker. Additionally, witnesses
report seeing Marco lurking near Maria's mansion on the
night of the murder, raising questions about his whereabouts and motives.

Sophia's personal diary is discovered at the crime scene, containing
cryptic entries alluding to feelings of jealousy and resentment towards Maria.
Additionally, witnesses report seeing Sophia arguing with Maria in public
on several occasions, suggesting a potential motive for the murder. Phone records
show a series of cryptic messages exchanged between Antonio and Maria in the hours
leading up to her murder, hinting at a possible motive related to their secretive
correspondence. Additionally, witnesses report seeing Antonio leaving Maria's mansion
shortly before the time of the murder, raising questions about his involvement in the crime.
